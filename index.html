About Us

At YourFash.ai, we are revolutionizing the way people shop for fashion online. Founded by Sarthak Batra and Shubhankar Sharma, we’re driven by the vision of creating a seamless platform for all your fashion needs—whether it’s trying on clothes, accessories, or anything else you can imagine.

Our Company

Sarthak Batra, who holds a PhD from the prestigious CVSSP, University of Surrey, and is an alumnus of IIT Kharagpur, brings a wealth of academic expertise and innovation. Shubhankar Sharma, a graduate of IIIT Bhopal, complements this with practical experience gained from working at top companies like Nvidia, SharChat, and Slice.

Our Virtual Try-On app uses the latest AI technology to provide users with a lifelike experience, allowing them to try garments and accessories in both 2D and 3D. We believe in bridging the gap between online and offline shopping, empowering our users to make confident fashion choices right from their devices. Our mission is simple: to redefine the way people interact with fashion, ensuring that everyone can find their perfect style, effortlessly. Join us on this exciting journey to transform the online shopping experience for fashion enthusiasts everywhere!
