About Us

At YourFash.ai, we are revolutionizing the way people shop for fashion online. Founded by Sarthak Batra and Shubhankar Sharma, we’re driven by the vision of creating a seamless platform for all your fashion needs—whether it’s trying on clothes, accessories, or anything else you can imagine.

Our Company

Sarthak Batra, who holds a PhD from the prestigious CVSSP, University of Surrey, and is an alumnus of IIT Kharagpur, brings a wealth of academic expertise and innovation. His deep understanding of cutting-edge technologies and research in AI is a cornerstone of our Virtual Try-On app.

Shubhankar Sharma, a graduate of IIIT Bhopal, complements this academic rigor with practical experience gained from working at top companies like Nvidia, SharChat, and Slice. His industry expertise ensures that our platform not only meets but exceeds the expectations of today's tech-savvy consumers.

Our Virtual Try-On app uses the latest AI technology to provide users with a lifelike experience, allowing them to try garments and accessories in both 2D and 3D. By leveraging advanced algorithms, we bridge the gap between online and offline shopping, giving users the confidence to make fashion choices from the comfort of their devices.

At the heart of our mission is a simple goal: to redefine the way people interact with fashion. We want to ensure that everyone can find their perfect style effortlessly. Join us on this exciting journey to transform the online shopping experience for fashion enthusiasts everywhere!
