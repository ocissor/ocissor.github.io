About Us

At YourFash.ai, we are revolutionizing the way people shop for fashion online. Founded by Sarthak Batra and Shubhankar Sharma, we’re driven by the vision of creating a seamless platform for all your fashion needs—whether it’s efficiently finding what you need or trying on clothes, accessories, or anything else you can imagine.

Our Company

At YourFash.ai, co-founders Sarthak Batra and Shubhankar Sharma are combining advanced research and industry expertise to revolutionize online shopping.

Sarthak Batra, currently pursuing a PhD at the Centre for Vision, Speech, and Signal Processing (CVSSP) at the University of Surrey, has published papers in top conferences, bringing cutting-edge academic rigor to our platform.

Shubhankar Sharma, a graduate of IIIT Bhopal, leverages practical experience from leading companies like Nvidia, SharChat, and Slice, ensuring our platform is both innovative and consumer-centric.

We are developing an intelligent recommendation system that seamlessly integrates with brand websites and dynamically adapts to each user’s preferences. One of our key features is a virtual try-on tool, allowing shoppers to see how outfits and accessories look on them in both 2D and 3D. This immersive experience empowers users to make more confident purchasing decisions.

Crucially, our system takes feedback from each virtual try-on session to refine future recommendations. When users see how items look on them, they gain clearer insights into what suits their preferences, which in turn provides strong cues for our system to fine-tune its suggestions. This continuous loop of feedback helps us offer increasingly personalized recommendations, evolving with each user's unique style and needs.
